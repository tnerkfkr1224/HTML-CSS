Description of company

Ceylon Textile Suppliers (CTS), an Australian owned organization based in Melbourne which supplies clothing related products to Australian and New Zealand retail outlets on a sale or return basis. It has an annual turnover of around $ 60M and plans to expand. And the same innovation that goes into our products also applies to our practices — strengthening our commitment to leave the world better than we found it. This is where your work can make a difference in people’s lives. Including your own. To support our growth, we have multiple openings available for passionate workers with varying degrees of experience to join us on our journey!
